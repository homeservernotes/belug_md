Welcome to the Bellevue Linux Users Group (BELUG) website.
BELUG is dedicated to promoting an interest in
and an understanding of [Linux](http://www.linfo.org/linuxdef.html)
and other [free software](http://www.linfo.org/free_software.html)
(New to Linux? Start [here](http://www.linfo.org/newbies.html))

BELUG - A friendly place to learn about Linux since February 2004!

[Meetings](meetings.html) are held on the second Tuesday of each month,
starting at 6:30PM.
We meet in-person at the
[North Bellevue Community Center](https://parks.bellevuewa.gov/community-centers/north-bellevue-community-center)
in room D
and on [Zoom](https://us02web.zoom.us/j/131116407?pwd=OFkwZzZWMVhDdlhMTU91K2lOVkQvZz09)
